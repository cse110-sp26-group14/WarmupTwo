# User Persona: Little Timmy

## Profile
**Name:** Little Timmy  
**Age:** 10 years old  
**Grade:** 5th Grade  

## Details
Little Timmy is a 10-year-old 5th grader who loves playing games on his Mom’s phone. Whenever she’s not around, he sneaks onto her device to play all sorts of games that provide quick fun and instant rewards. Comfortable with mobile apps and technology, Timmy is easily drawn to these experiences and spends as much time as he can playing. He dreams of getting his hands on the brand new video game console that just came out, which costs a staggering $800.

## Wants and Needs
* **Goal-Oriented Rewards:** He wants an app that gives him rewards that might help him earn enough money to get the console he dreams of. 
* **Aesthetic Preferences:** He is drawn to games that are fun and engaging, with flashy visuals and themes similar to his favorite games like *Among Us* and *Fortnite*. 
* **Usability:** He prefers a simple, easy-to-navigate interface.
* **Positive Reinforcement:** He enjoys rewards that are frequent and immediate, making him feel like he is constantly winning.

## Pain Points (Antagonizers)
* **Loss Aversion:** Little Timmy hates losing and not receiving any rewards. When this happens, he quickly becomes upset and may cry if it occurs too often. 
* **Boredom:** He gets frustrated with slow or boring games.
* **Complexity:** He dislikes complicated interfaces.
* **Lack of Feedback:** He is discouraged by anything that doesn’t give him frequent, exciting feedback or rewards.
