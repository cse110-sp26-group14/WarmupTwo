# CSE 110 - Basic Slot Machine Research

## 1. Technical Jargon (The Logic)
The underlying math and logic that govern how a slot machine functions:

* **RTP (Return to Player):** The percentage of wagered money paid back to players over time (e.g., 96%).
* **Volatility:** The risk level of the game. **High Volatility** offers rare but large wins, while **Low Volatility** provides frequent, smaller payouts.
* **RNG (Random Number Generator):** Known as the "brain," this ensures every spin result is truly random.
* **Hit Frequency:** A metric indicating how often any winning combination occurs.
* **Paylines / Ways:** The specific paths symbols must take to trigger a win (e.g., "243 Ways to Win").
* **Reel Set:** The physical grid dimensions, commonly seen as $5\times3$ or $6\times4$.

---

## 2. Core Features (The Gameplay)
Mechanics designed to keep gameplay engaging and dynamic:

* **Wilds:** Substitutes for other symbols to create wins; can be **Sticky**, **Expanding**, or **Shifting**.
* **Scatters:** Unique symbols that trigger bonuses or Free Spins regardless of their position on the reels.
* **Cascading Reels:** A mechanic where winning symbols disappear and are replaced by new symbols dropping from above.
* **Multipliers:** Mathematical boosters that increase win amounts (e.g., $2\times$, $5\times$, $10\times$).
* **Hold & Win:** A feature where specific symbols are locked in place to potentially trigger a jackpot.
* **Gamble Feature:** An optional "double or nothing" mini-game accessible after a win.

### App Elements (UI/UX)
* **The HUD:** Includes the balance display, bet size adjustment, and the Spin button.
* **Paytable:** The reference menu explaining symbol values and game rules.
* **Near-Miss Effect:** Audio-visual tension used when two scatters land to build excitement for the third.
* **Big Win Sequence:** High-energy celebrations involving fountaining coins and screen shaking.

---

## 4. User Types
Understanding the target demographic for specific game designs:

* **Casuals:** Play for fun/distraction; they generally prefer bright colors and low volatility.
* **High Rollers:** Players seeking high stakes, high volatility, and exclusive VIP perks.
* **Grinders:** Motivated by "Level Up" progression systems and daily missions.
* **Social Players:** Value community features like leaderboards, team challenges, and gifting coins.
